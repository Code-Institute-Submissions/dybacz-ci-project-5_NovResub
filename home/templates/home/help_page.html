Help & FAQs

How can I contact you for help?

- If you have any questions at all, please contact us by email on fitness.peak.gb@gmail.com

Do you have a guarantee on your products?

- All our products come with a 12 month manufacturer's guarantee as standard.

How can I get a voucher?

- We regularly send up-to date voucher codes out via our email newsletters, make sure to subscribe on our home page.

How do I redeem a voucher?

- All valid vouchers can be entered on the secure checout page, if you are still having any issues please contact us by email on fitness.peak.gb@gmail.com

What happens if I have a problem with my order?

- If you have any problems at all please contact us on fitness.peak.gb@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all emails within 2-3 working days.

What if I receive a faulty item?

- If you receive a faulty product please send us an email on fitness.peak.gb@gmail.com so we can resolve this for you. We aim to respond to all emails within 2-3 working days.

What is your returns policy?

- You can return a product providing it is unused within 28 days of the order dispatch date.

How do I change or update my details?

- You can update all your details via the 'My Profile' page on the 'Account' menu on the home page.

I'm having trouble logging into my account.

- Make sure you are logging in with the same email address and password you registered with. If you've forgotten your password then click 'Forgotten Password' on the login page and follow the instructions.

How can I become a supplier for Peak Fitness?

- Thank you for your interest in becoming a supplier for us! As a growing business we are always on the hunt for new suppliers. Please drop us an email on account.fitness.peak.gb@gmail.com to discuss further.
