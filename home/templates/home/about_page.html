About Us

Peak Fitness is an independent business in the North West of England. We pride ourselves in being a friendly and approachable team of fitness enthusiasts and endeavour to encourage others to take their fitness to the next level by providing everything you need to get you there.

We offer personalised subscription plans for exercise and nutrition, meaning that you no longer have to conform to standards set for others - you can tailor them to you!

Here at Peak Fitness we believe that good health shouldn't just be a wish, it should be a given. Our ethos is to provide affordable fitness equipment and accessories in a sustainable way by ensuring we only work with other planet-friendly, UK independent businesses. By respecting our planet as much as we respect our health, we have been able to pave the way for a shift in the market, meaning that when you purchase something from Peak Fitness you are not only buying into a healthier you, but a healthier planet too.

Join the community today and let's help you reach your Peak Fitness together!
